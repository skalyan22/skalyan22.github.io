Athletics

Soccer. Track and Field.

Introduction:

As stated in my home page, I play club soccer and run track. I've been playing club soccer for nearly 9 years and I currently play for the Michigan Hawks ECNL team. I am so lucky to get the opportunity to play for the club year round and travel as a team to places all over the country for events. In addition, I run track and field for my high school during the spring season. My goal is to play both soccer and track at the collegiate level and continue doing what I love for another 4 years after high school. Keep scrolling to learn more about each sport.




My Soccer Story:

I started playing club soccer about 9 years ago for our local travel soccer team. At first it was just a hobby and sport that I loved to play and travel playing. Fast-forward 5 years, my parents get a call from the Michigan Hawks club--which is one of the best soccer clubs in the nation--and asks me to attend some of their practices and tryout for the team. 3 years later, I'm playing soccer and aiming to play at the collegiate level. I get to travel across the nation with my beloved team and play for college coaches at California, Florida, Arizona, New Jersey, and more. The friendships I made will be lifelong and I do not regret the decision I made three years ago to switch clubs for it is one of the best things to happen to me in my teenage years. My Track Story:

I started running track in middle school during 7th and 8th grade and eventually even placed in the Oakland Country track meet in 8th grade. My best season so far has been during freshman year of high school. I run sprints and I usually run in multiple events including the 100m dash, 200m dash, 400m dash and some relays including the 4 x 100m relay, 4 x 200m relay, and 4 x 400m relay. In just one season of track and field, I managed to earn my varsity letter, earn 11 medals from meets, honored as an All-League runner, earn the Rookie of the Year award, and best of all, broke two school records that haven't been broke in over 20 years. It was only after my success in Track freshmen year that I realized how much I loved the sport and want to hopefully continue playing it in college. I broke the 400m dash record as well as the 4 x 100m relay with my relay team. Another goal I have in Track is to place at States which I hope to accomplish next year.
